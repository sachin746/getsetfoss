Our Motivation

Our Goals

A desire to help and empower others between community contributors in technology
began to grow in 2018.

Awareness

Learning cannot be barred when the source is open. With Get Set FOSS!, Axios, the technical club of IIIT Lucknow aims to acquaint the young and fresh developers of the country with this vast ocean of learning - THE OPEN SOURCE.

Github

Open source and its learning has got some true prerequisites- and the most important of all of them are Git and GitHub. The event will not only aim to help the participants learn these tools, but also realize how big they are when it comes to the usage.

Community Building

A developer can grow when his community is strong. With the aim to unite various developers across the institute, the event will serve its purpose strengthening the existing community and bringing more like-minded people together.

Improve Skills

The best part of open source is the learning, it brings along. The event will have projects from various domains, which can help the participants grow in various areas of technology and explore their interests at the same time.
